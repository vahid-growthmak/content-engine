* Light grey body copy: `#5B6470` / `#6B7280`  
* Section dividers / soft backgrounds: `#F5F7FA` / `#F3F4F6`  
* Success/check icons: brand blue \+ green tones

**Usage rule:** Lead with white space \+ blue. The multi-color accents are reserved for data visualizations, the Growth Engine motif, and infographic-style elements — not for body UI.

## **2\. Typography**

The site uses a clean **geometric/grotesque sans-serif** throughout (consistent with a modern Webflow B2B build — likely Inter, Poppins, or a similar humanist/geometric sans). Recommended system:

| Level | Style | Specs |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| H1 / Hero | Bold sans, tight tracking, large scale | \~56–72px, weight 700, two-tone (navy \+ blue accent line) |
| H2 / Section titles | Semi-bold | \~32–40px, weight 600 |
| Eyebrow labels ("WHO WE WORK WITH", "WHY CHOOSE US") | Uppercase, letter-spaced, blue/grey | \~13–14px, weight 600, tracking \+1px |
| Body | Regular sans | \~16–18px, weight 400, grey `#5B6470`, generous line-height (\~1.6) |
| Stat callouts ("3.2x", "$12M+", "5x") | Extra-bold | Large, navy/blue, paired with small caption label |
| Buttons | Medium/semi-bold | \~15–16px, weight 500–600 |

**Pairing recommendation:** One sans-serif family across the brand (e.g., **Poppins/Inter** for headings, **Inter** for body) to keep the modern, tech-forward, approachable tone. Avoid serifs and decorative fonts.

## **3\. Visual Style & Identity**

**Overall mood:** Modern, clean, tech-forward, trustworthy, data-driven. Lots of white space; nothing cluttered.

* **Logo:** Ascending blue arrow/mountain mark inside a circle \+ "GROWTHMAK" wordcap in navy. Reversed (white) version used on dark footer. The upward arrow reinforces the "growth" theme.\[[growthmak](https://growthmak.com/)\]  
* **Signature motif:** The circular **"Growth Engine" wheel** (Foundation → Traffic → Conversion → Operations → Social Proof) with a central metric hub. This is the brand's hero visual device and should anchor decks, ads, and explainer graphics.  
* **Buttons:** Pill/rounded-rectangle shape. Two variants — solid blue (primary) and white with blue outline (secondary) — both with a trailing arrow icon.\[[growthmak](https://growthmak.com/)\]  
* **Iconography:** Simple line \+ filled icons, circular check marks for feature lists, consistent blue/teal accents.  
* **Cards:** Rounded corners, soft shadows, white surfaces on light grey sections — used for services, projects, case studies, and blog posts.  
* **Imagery:** Real client/founder photos for testimonials and partner logos; clean logo grids for social proof. Credibility badges (Clutch, Startup India, IISc/NSRCEL) presented in a tidy row.  
* **Data emphasis:** Big bold numbers ("3.2x", "$5M+", "$12M+", "2.12x ROAS") are a core visual hallmark — proof-driven design. Lean into oversized stat typography.

## **4\. Quick Do / Don't**

**Do:** Use generous white space; lead with blue \+ navy; use the Growth Engine wheel as the signature graphic; emphasize big proof-stats; keep one sans-serif family; use rounded pill CTAs with arrows.

**Don't:** Overload the accent rainbow into UI chrome; mix in serif or decorative fonts; crowd layouts; use low-contrast grey text on colored backgrounds.
